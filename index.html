About

I am a PhD candidate in Statistics in the Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science at the University of Waterloo, supervised by Professor Ali Ghodsi. I am also a Faculty Affiliate at the Vector Institute. My research interests include deep learning theory, Bayesian deep learning, and variational inference.

You can find a list of my research work here.

Before beginning my PhD, I completed a Master of Mathematics in Statistics at Waterloo. I also completed a Bachelor of Mathematics, majoring in Statistics and minoring in Computer Science.
